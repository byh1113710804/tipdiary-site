TipDiary Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

The short version

We collect nothing. TipDiary has no accounts, no analytics SDKs, no ads, and no servers of ours that ever see your data.

Where your data lives

Everything you log (shifts, tips, hours, jobs, profile settings) is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync (optional), your data is stored in your own iCloud private database, managed by Apple and protected by your Apple ID. We cannot access it.

What leaves your device

  • Nothing is sent to us. The app periodically downloads a public tax-rules file (anonymous, standard web request, no identifiers attached).
  • Purchases are processed entirely by Apple (StoreKit). We never see payment details.
  • Exports (CSV/PDF) are created on-device and shared only where you choose to send them.

Data retention & deletion

Delete the app (and its iCloud data via iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud) and your data is gone. We keep no copies — we never had any.

Analytics & crash reports

We use no third-party analytics. If you opted in to Apple's "Share With App Developers", Apple may provide us anonymized crash reports through the standard Apple developer tools.

Not tax advice

TipDiary provides estimates based on current IRS/Treasury guidance. It is not tax, legal, or accounting advice.
